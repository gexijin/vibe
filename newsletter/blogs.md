# Top Bioinformatics and Genomics Blogs

This list contains influential blogs and news sources in the bioinformatics, genomics, and computational biology field.

## 1. Bits of DNA
- **Author**: Lior Pachter (Professor of Computational Biology, Caltech)
- **URL**: https://liorpachter.wordpress.com/
- **Focus**: RNA-seq, single-cell genomics, computational biology methods
- **Why influential**: Critical analysis of bioinformatics methods, high-impact research commentary

## 2. Opiniomics
- **Author**: Mick Watson (Professor, University of Edinburgh)
- **URL**: http://www.opiniomics.org/
- **Focus**: Genomics, metagenomics, bioinformatics best practices
- **Why influential**: Practical bioinformatics insights, data analysis commentary

## 3. Living in an Ivory Basement
- **Author**: C. Titus Brown (Professor, UC Davis)
- **URL**: http://ivory.idyll.org/blog/
- **Focus**: Open science, software development, genomics, training
- **Why influential**: Strong advocate for reproducibility and open source bioinformatics

## 4. Genomes Unzipped
- **Authors**: Multiple contributors (genomics researchers)
- **URL**: http://www.genomesunzipped.org/
- **Focus**: Personal genomics, genetics, genomics research
- **Why influential**: Diverse perspectives on genomics and its applications

## 5. Omics! Omics!
- **Author**: Keith Robison (Computational Biologist)
- **URL**: https://omicsomics.blogspot.com/
- **Focus**: Sequencing technologies, genomics news, computational biology
- **Why influential**: Timely coverage of new sequencing platforms and genomics developments
